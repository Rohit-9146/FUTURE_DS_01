## 2. Business Insights & Actionable Recommendations

### Key Business Insights
- A small number of products contribute a large portion of total revenue.
- Sales show fluctuations over time, indicating seasonal demand.
- The Technology category is the most profitable.
- The West region generates the highest profit.

### Actionable Recommendations
- Focus marketing and inventory efforts on Technology products.
- Prioritize expansion and customer retention in the West region.
- Plan promotions during low-sales periods to reduce revenue fluctuations.
- Review low-profit categories to optimize pricing or costs.
